About Us

The Computer Science Club of Solano Community College was founded on August 12th, 2024, by our President, Jennifer Petsko. Since then, our club has grown tremendously, amassing over 100 Discord members, surpassing 50 official members, and regularly hosting well-attended meetings.

Our Purpose

The Computer Science Club of Solano Community College is dedicated to creating a supportive, engaging, and educational environment for students interested in all areas of computing and technology. Our mission is to engage in collaboration, spark innovation, and build a strong community based on projects, hackathons, and regular meetings.

Our membership speaks for itself in showing the possibilities we can achieve together. Through our active Discord server and growing community, we aim to make computer science inclusive, approachable, and exciting for all attending students.
