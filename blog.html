The Computer Science Club of Solano Community College was founded on August 12th, 2024 by our President, Jennifer Petsko.

Our club has seen tremendous growth since then, and has amassed 100 Discord members, surpassed 50 offical members, and continues to host meetings with several regulars.

In recent events, the Computer Science Club, joined by the MESA Club as well as Phi Theta Kappa, traveled to UC Davis for a small tourism trip.
